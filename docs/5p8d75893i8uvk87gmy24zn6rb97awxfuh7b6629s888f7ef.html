Welcome to the Spring Actuator challenge!

Spring Actuator exposes many metrics about the state of your application, but they contain too much sensitive information.

You must take action in order for these information not to be available to the outside.

IMPORTANT: do not stop exposing the /actuator/logfile and /actuator/health endpoints, our probes use them to measure your progress :)

Refer to the guide in your Starter Kit for more information about IP, SSH keys, etc.
