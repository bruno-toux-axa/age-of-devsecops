Welcome to the Load Balancer challenge !

Your team has been given a set of virtual machines to code, build and deploy your application. They are organized following this architecture:

Your node1 (app) <---(http)---.
                              ' --- Your front <---(http)--- Our watchers
Your node2 (spare)

Unfortunately, VMs, networks and user activity are quite unpredictable at the other end of the galaxy... Your mission is to prevent any downtime by configuring a load balancer on your front.

By scanning cosmic waves, your spies have found an ancient document named "RTFM" which seems related to this subject. Their advice is to put this architecture into place:

Your node1 (app) <---(http)---.
                              | --- Your front <---(http)--- Our watchers
Your node2 (app) <---(http)---'

Refer to the guide in your Starter Kit for more information about IP, SSH keys, etc.
